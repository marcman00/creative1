Realizing the Creative Genius Within Us

There is a creative genius inside of each of us. Too many people don't realize this and as such, don't even try to tap into it. Once we realize this we can focus on developing abilities that we hadn't before even realized we had.

Resilience: Pushing through Challenges

Sometimes we face challenges that seems overwhelming. We can either surrender to our circumstances or find ways to go over or around them. Often, we will find that helping others through their challenges gives us more strength to get through our own.
